How It Works
Multi-Layer AI Analysis

Six detection modules work in parallel to catch forgeries that human eyes might miss.

🔍

OCR Text Extraction

Advanced optical character recognition extracts all visible text, detecting anomalies in content, dates, and degree nomenclature.

🏛️

Institution Validation

Cross-references issuing organizations against accredited university and certification body databases worldwide.

✍️

Signature Analysis

Inspects printed and digital signatures for authenticity markers and compares against known signing authority patterns.

⚠️

Forgery Pattern Detection

Catches font substitution, pixel artifacts around edited text, seal duplication, and common template-cloning techniques.

📐

Layout & Format Check

Analyzes document proportions, seal placement, margin consistency, and formatting standards against institutional norms.

📊

Confidence Scoring

Produces a granular confidence score with fully explainable findings — no black box. Every flag is shown with reasoning.
